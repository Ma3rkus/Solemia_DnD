## Snippet 1

Dear Diary, 

When I was younger, I kept a journal almost every day. It feels strange to start again after so many years. But [[Gresha]] and [[Grindy]] insisted, and honestly… it might not be a bad idea. If we succeed, perhaps these pages will help tell the truth of what happened. 

Today we finally found [[Vaelor Kain]]. The notes from his hideout led us toward the Stonewake mountains. Deep within one of the old dwarven vaults lies an artifact the dwarves call the Heartshard a relic said to resonate with divine energy. 

[[Vaelor Kain]] was there when we arrived. For a brief moment, we actually restrained him. Chains of divine light, roots from [[Gresha]]’s magic, even [[Grindy]]’s ridiculous bear hug. But then he spoke. Not like a madman. Like... a grieving man. He told us the world runs on a thread of fate. That once he had a life, a beautiful one. He had a wife. She was cursed with a disease that could not be cured. Magic failed. Science failed. The priests told him to pray. Trust in divine will. Wait for a miracle. But none came. He told us he held her as she died. And the last thing she said was: _"Do not hate the world for losing me. Please live enough for both of us."_ 

[[Vaelor Kain]] said that was the moment he understood something. If mortals create divine power through belief… why should mortals not wield it themselves? Before we could react, he grabbed the artifact. The air exploded with power. Light twisted. Stone cracked. The chains shattered. And he vanished. 

[[Seraphine Crowe]] believes he is preparing the final ritual. Tomorrow we move again. The Flame guides us. I only hope we are not already too late.
