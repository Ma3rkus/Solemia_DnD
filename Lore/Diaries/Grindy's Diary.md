## Snippet 1
Dear diary, 

Gresha… I’m sorry it took me so long to write again. 

You said we should keep writing, so people would know what really happened. But I did not know how to put it into words. 

I still don’t. 

Everything happened so fast. There was light everywhere. Caldus was praying. Seraphine was shouting something I could not hear. Then the world felt like it broke. 

When the light was gone, Vaelor was gone. Seraphine too. Caldus was on the ground. I tried to wake him. But he was cold and pale. 

People are calling us heroes now. But I do not feel like one. I just miss my friends....
