## Snippet 1
Dear Diary,

It has been a while since I last wrote something like this. With everything happening because of Vaelor Kain, none of us have had much time to think let alone write. Still, I managed to convince the others that keeping a record might be wise.  
If we fail… perhaps someone will know what truly happened.

Convincing Grindy was easy! He loved the idea immediately. Once he started talking about how future bards might sing about us, [[Caldus Crowe]] laughed and agreed. [[Seraphine Crowe]] rolled her eyes but I think she enjoyed that thought.

A few days ago we found Vaelor’s old refuge. Shelves filled with burned books, torn pages scattered across the floor, strange symbols carved into stone.  
Some texts spoke of things I wish I had never read. Manifesting a god. Binding divine essence. Dragging something eternal into the mortal world. I do not know if Vaelor has gone mad or if he truly believes this is possible.

[[Seraphine Crowe]] studied what was left of the writings and tried to find out where he could be. [[Caldus Crowe]] says we will find him and stop whatever ritual he is planning. I want to believe him. But something about those notes made my stomach turn. At first I would think that such things would be impossible. But the precise research made it seem, possible.... 

By the Blooming Mother, I hope we reach him before he finishes whatever he has begun.
