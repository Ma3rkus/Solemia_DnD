# Fintan Saoirse Fionn

## Description
 Fintan is a woodland-elf warlock of elven middleage, 869 (Human years +- 32)
 
 In general he looks like a healthy, maybe lean/skinny adult elf. Skin complexity is light brown as is expected for elves of the woodland variety. He walks with a cane, but has no limp. When you look at him form bottom up, he looks athletic build, almost skidish. Instead of the usual archery that is associated with the woodelves, Fintan is specialized in melee combat. 
 
 He has a component pouch on one side of his belt and on the other side he has a leather pouch containing a special edition playing cards. We have been delaying the inevitable. When looking eye to eye with Fintan, there are a couple of things that catch the eye (Literally). First of all he wears a mask covering most of his face, except the right upper part. The eyes, nose and mouth is kept clear aswell. This mask is made from wood. When looking at his eyes the most significant feature of fintan becomes apparant. Eventhough one would not notice by his way of walking & talking, Fintan is fully blind. His eyes are a very light blue hue, almost white and very cloudy. The main reason for the mask is the scar covering his entire face including eye. The eye that is not covered has a continuous tear forming in the corner that is kind of feeding the mask. There is a small leaf forming in the cracks of the mask, nourished by the tears. 
 
 His hair would naturally be a mix between black, white an different hues of gray. But he prefers to color it brown green. When you spot Fintan for the first time you immediatly notice a military disciple in his way of walking and standing. When you ask him he will deny any affiliation to any military.
 
 He has a tattoo starting behind his right ear running towarsds his neck.

### Tattoos
##### Appearance

The tattoo is called “Caoineadh na Crann” — _The Lament of the Tree_.

It is burned and carved into the skin using a freshly charred splinter of the Crann Bethadh (Tree of Life). The process leaves a scar-tattoo rather than ink.

##### Design elements

The tattoo contains three symbolic parts:

1. The Spiral Roots  
A triple spiral at the base representing:

- Life
- Death
- The unseen path between them

This symbol reflects the belief that members of the Guard walk between worlds—the living realm and the realm of spirits.

2. The Silent Branch  
A single branch extending upward with seven small thorns.

These represent the seven oaths sworn by members of the Guard:

1. Silence
2. Loyalty
3. Balance
4. Vigilance
5. Mercy when deserved
6. Death when required
7. Duty before self
8. The Hollow Leaf

At the top sits a leaf shape with a small hollow center.

This empty space represents the voice of the banshee—a sound that cannot be seen, only heard.

##### Placement

The tattoo is typically placed:

- Along the collarbone or
- On the shoulder blade

Fintan’s tattoo is slightly unusual.

Because of Tuatha Dé Danann’s influence, his mark was carved behind his right ear and along the neck, allowing him to feel the raised scar lines when he touches it.

This was done intentionally.

The tattoo itself became a tactile map, something he could read with his fingers.

### Mask
Name: Foscadh na Crann - _"The Shelter of the Tree"_
##### Origin
When Fintan’s father restored his honor, he commissioned the mask from a forest warden-artisan, a rare craft tradition among the woodland elves.
The mask is carved from fallen bark of the Crann Bethadh.

**Important detail:**  
The bark was never cut from a living tree.
In elven belief, cutting bark from the Tree of Life would curse the wearer.

Instead, the bark must be found naturally fallen during a storm.
##### Cultural Meaning
Among woodland elves, the mask carries several meanings:
###### 1. Restoration of Honor
The mask signifies that someone has returned from disgrace but bears the memory of it.
The scars are not hidden — they are integrated.

###### 2. Silence and Watchfulness
Because the mask partially covers the mouth, it symbolizes a warrior who speaks little but sees (or senses) much.

###### 3. Guardian of the Forest
The bark symbolizes a living bond with the Crann Bethadh, implying the wearer acts as a protector of the natural order.

## Timeline of Life
### Chapter 1: Youth - Start of a dark life (384 - 481) 
 Born in the year 384 after the yellow leaves. as the second son of the house of Fionn. In the year 389 Fintan wass playing outside with his brothers. They were playing in the forest with the halfing neighbours. When at a certain moment in time Fintan fell on an exposed tree root. He only knows this part, because it was told to him. But he ripped a part his face and lost sight in his right eye. During the healing process, due to a mistake from his brother who mixed up some flowers. He lost his sight completely. For him the years following the accident he learned to embrace his other senses. But people called him Súileabháin, or One-eyed as only one eye was visible. This name has a very bad after-taste for Fintan, but he will adopt this name later as pseudonym. He will wear different masks through-out his life, to cover-up the wounds. 
### Chapter 2: Adolesence - Seeing the light, only to reach the dark (481 - 807) 
 Once he became adolesence, he had the oportunity to train under Tuatha Dé Danann. She was the part of the high command of [[Cliodhna]]. Her division the Guard of the Banshee. This is an elitegroup of elven fighters. Reconizable by their identifying tattoo. This tattoo is created by carving the known form with a freshly burned piece of wood of the Crann Bethadh (Tree of life). He learned to augment his senses to behave indistuigishable from others. During that training he learned to fight aswell. But he does not share much information about this training, due to the secrecy regarding the [[Cliodhna]]. He learns [[Thieves' Cant]] as part of his training with the Cliodhna. After this he returned home to work as a tracker for the hunt. A friend of his introduced him to the cardgaming world and gambling. This friend was Siud Fennigan, a human who was at the time a smith apprentice. Siud became addicted and betrayed Fintan for a sum of money. They drugged him, used him as a slave. He does not know how they look or who they are. But there voice still aunts him in his dreams.To mediate the abuse, he started brewing and drinking in excess. When he eventually release, he was an alcoholic and generally becoming a bad representation of his family. He doesn't like talking about this. He was cast out by his family and made to change his name to Dorcha (Meaning Dark blue, bad connotation in woodland elven society). 
### Chapter 3: Adult life - Reconsiliation & a Patron (807 - 1253)
 When he turned 425, he was living of what he could find in the forest. He fully embraced the wild nature, seeing this as a possible way to redeem himself in the eyes of his gods (Cernunnos & Mor-Rioghan). He returned home. Empty handed but with renewed energy to prove his worth. He started to work for his family again. Starting at the bottom of the social ladder. Working his way up, eventually he had received the raven seal. This is an invitation to a try-out of a secret special unit, [[Craobh Ruadh]]. Only the name of this unit is known, but even that is unsure. He would train there and receive the role of tracker for High Stakes missions. But he would never go to such missions, as he was called back to his family. His father was very ill and as a last act of reconciliation wanted to restore his honour. 
 For this restoration, he received 2 gifts: 
 * A mask covering the left part and the lower right part of his face, made with a piece of bark of the Tree of Life. 
 * The chance to adopt his original family name 

 After his time with the [[Craobh Ruadh]] and due to the death of his father, he retired. He remained active in service of his family. But after a 200 years the adventure started calling. So he started to make himself available as security companion. He had a big advantage, he could enforce a high level of secrecy because of his blindness. During one of his jobs he was ambushed and he fought until he lost conscience. When waking up he discovered he was in a guild house. He was found by a merchant guild. They took care of his wounds. During his recovery he had fever dreams. In one of those he was approached by his latest customer. He could see her... She mentioned she was able to survive thanks to his endurance. She wanted to thank him, so she lifted her hood. There was no person underneed the hood. She was [[Lady luck]] herself and propsed a deal ... a [[Pact & Patron|pact]], a [[The contract|contract]]: Keep the services running and she would grand him additional abilities. When he woke up, he had a; according to the guild master; a highly detailed; deck of cards in his hands. He began to exhibit some magic powers. In exchange for those powers he kept his promise and served people to ensure their safety during travels. A generic theme in his live is that he overcomes everything and every scar or flaw he makes a part of his way of handling. (Card addiction becomes source of magic, blindness becomes augmented senses, ...)
