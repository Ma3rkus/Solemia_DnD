## William - Wout
- ## Sinderclasp
    
    Wondrous Item, Uncommon (requires attunement) A black-iron right gauntlet with narrow cracks across its knuckles. Enchanted Fist. Your Unarmed Strikes made with this gauntlet are magical and deal 1d8 Bludgeoning damage plus your Strength modifier. Ignite. As a Bonus Action, you activate the gauntlet for 1 minute. It becomes wreathed in harmless flames, and its Unarmed Strikes deal an additional 1d6 Fire damage. Once used, Ignite can’t be used again until the next dawn.
    
* ## Rimegrip
    
    Wondrous Item, Uncommon (requires attunement) A pale-steel left gauntlet that becomes covered in blue-white ice when activated. Enchanted Fist. Your Unarmed Strikes made with this gauntlet are magical and deal 1d8 Bludgeoning damage plus your Strength modifier. Freeze. As a Bonus Action, you activate the gauntlet for 1 minute. Its Unarmed Strikes deal an additional 1d6 Cold damage. Once per turn when you deal this Cold damage, the target’s Speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn. Once used, Freeze can’t be used again until the next dawn.
    
	**Wearing Both Gauntlets**
    
    When both gauntlets are active: You gain Resistance to Fire and Cold damage. If you hit one creature with each gauntlet during the same turn, it must make a DC 15 Constitution save. On a failure, thermal shock deals 2d8 Force damage, halves its Speed, and prevents Reactions until the start of your next turn. Thermal shock can occur only once per turn. Together, the gauntlets count as a Rare magic-item set, but still consume two attunement slots.
