#  🏛 The Major Regions

##  ☀️ High Solis

A radiant capital city built on order, discipline, and legacy. Three major political forces compete for influence:

- **The Enlightened**: reform-minded thinkers
- **The Knights of the Sun**: militant defenders of divine order
- **The Hand of Sol**: loyalists to Caldus’ legacy

The wealthy houses still hold most power. Lower districts struggle for representation. High Solis values strength, unity, and reputation. (Similar to Piltover vs Zaun for my Arcane plebs) 

Open plains - small forests

---

##  🌲 Evergreen

A vast forested region of villages, druid circles, and old traditions. Years ago, the Four eliminated a dangerous apex predator threatening settlements. Now:

- Marshlands expand.
- Bullywogs multiply.
- Druids question whether the intervention upset something deeper.

Evergreen values balance, memory, and quiet resilience. 

---

## ⛰ Stonewake Holds

Mountain strongholds carved into stone. Clan loyalty runs deep sometimes too deep. The Legendary Four helped shatter the Oathforge, a ritual stone that once magically bound dwarves into war-service. Freedom followed. So did fractured unity. Stonewake values endurance, honor, and personal strength. 

---

##  🌊 Shattercoast

Trade-heavy coastal cities built on wealth and ambition. The Four helped end slavery here. But the systems behind it evolved instead of disappearing. Debt contracts. Indentured labor. Prison fleets. The Shattercoast values opportunity, influence, and survival.
