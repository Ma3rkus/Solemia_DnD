# Welcome to Solemia
Sixty years ago, the world was nearly torn apart.
A mortal sorcerer attempted to kill an aspect of the First Flame the divine force associated with Life itself. He believed mortals should shape their own destiny, not distant gods.

Four adventurers stopped him.
The ritual collapsed.
One hero died.
One vanished.
The world was saved.

Statues were raised.
Songs were written.
History moved on.

You grew up hearing those names.

Caldus Crowe: The Hero.
Seraphine Crowe: The Tactician.
Grindry: The Goat of the Mountain.
Gresha: Unchained.

They were not born legends.
They were adventurers.
Like you.

---

# The World Today
Solemia is not at war.
But it is not calm.

Politics sharpen.
Faith intensifies.
Nature behaves strangely in places.
Old rivalries resurface.

People feel that something is shifting beneath ordinary life.

No one agrees on what.

---
# 🏛 The Major Regions
## ☀️ Solcast dominion
### High Solis:
A radiant capital city built on order, discipline, and legacy.

Three major political forces compete for influence:

**The Enlightened:** reform-minded thinkers
**The Knights of the Sun:** militant defenders of divine order
**The Hand of Sol:** loyalists to Caldus’ legacy

The wealthy houses still hold most power.
Lower districts struggle for representation.

High Solis values strength, unity, and reputation.

---
## 🌲 Evergreen
A vast forested region of villages, druid circles, and old traditions.

Years ago, the Four eliminated a dangerous apex predator threatening settlements.

Now:

- Marshlands expand.
- Bullywogs multiply.
- Druids question whether the intervention upset something deeper.

Evergreen values balance, memory, and quiet resilience.

---
## ⛰ Stonewake Holds
Mountain strongholds carved into stone.

Clan loyalty runs deep sometimes too deep.
The Legendary Four helped shatter the Oathforge, a ritual stone that once magically bound dwarves into war-service.
Freedom followed.
So did fractured unity.

Stonewake values endurance, honor, and personal strength.

---
## 🌊 Shattercoast
Trade-heavy coastal cities built on wealth and ambition.

The Four helped end slavery here.
But the systems behind it evolved instead of disappearing.

Debt contracts.
Indentured labor.
Prison fleets.

The Shattercoast values opportunity, influence, and survival.

# Deities
Ying & Yang, Life & Death, Up & Down, Cold & Warm. Many have different interpretations of the gods. Sol, Mother Nature, Everlight, the First Flame. Luna, Necros, the Endless Night

The First Flame is associated with:
- Life
- Growth
- Passion
- Light

The Endless night is associated with:
- Death
- Memory
- Cold
- Inevitability

Temples exist across Solemia.
Clerics, paladins, and believers are common.

Faith is woven into society but interpreted differently depending on region.
Most people do not question the gods.

Some do.
